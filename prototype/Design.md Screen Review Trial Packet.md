## Minimum evidence checklist

- [ ] Screen links back to the exact `DESIGN.md` recipe or section used.
- [ ] At least three recipe-specific nouns are visible in the UI.
- [ ] Primary CTA names the next concrete agent/user action.
- [ ] At least one proof metric is above generic activity counters.
- [ ] One generic baseline delta is written before acceptance.
- [ ] Any missing evidence is marked as a blocker, not a pass.
